Ada Lovelace

First Computer Programmer

A summary of his biography

Currently know as Ada Lovelace, was a mathematician and writer. She entered in the history mainly for writing the first algorithm which was processed by a machine, the Charles Babbage's analytical machine.

In addition to several medals and honors created under his name, was designated october 9, 2018 as National Ada Lovelace Day in United States of America, honoring the life and legacy of the first computer programmer.

In today the developers worldwide are grateful for his genius and can make codes more easily.

"I am never as happy as when I am really committed to something, and that makes me wonderfully joyous and at other times rejoicing, which is curious and very satisfying." _ Ada Lovelace
